About the Webpage

In the project I was tasked to create a webpage for various famous musical composers. This webpage consists of a homepage and pages for each of the five composers. Once you click on a composer you will be taken to the corrisponding page where you can learn more about them. You can then return to the homepage or click on the other composers names in the navigation bar.
